About

Welcome to the site! I hope your time here will give you some indication of who I am as well as the dedication and thought that I put into my work.

At my previous position I had the luck of testing and structuring learning platforms as the orginaztion was expanding to make professional development more accessible to clients. During this time I had a chance to play around with HTML and CSS, but as I delved in deeper I wanted to change the platform even more. I wanted to tailor it to meet our clients needs.

As I would learn later in General Assembly's bootcamp, I was looking for APIs, ways to simplify navigation, and upgrading tools to meet the audience's needs. With a fervor that I hadn't felt in a long time I started to code, first using code academy and other resources, but then deciding to make a career move.

And here I am, coding to my heart's content.
